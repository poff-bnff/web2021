    The Rejected Shorts Picture Show

    Local title
    The Rejected Shorts Picture Show
    Festival
    PÖFF Shorts 2020
    Programme
    Shorts Alternatives

    Join us for a journey around the world through films that didn’t find their place in it – and for good reason. In this carefully handpicked selection of "bad" short films rejected elsewhere, scarred but brave filmmakers face their fears and unveil their own monstrous creations for you to enjoy. Featuring, but not limited to, absolute travesties of terrible technical execution, painfully obvious budget limitations, questionable scriptwriting, pompous directing, and most importantly, at its core, a pure, burning passion to make films.
