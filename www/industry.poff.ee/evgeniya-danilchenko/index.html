Evgeniya Danilchenko

Head of creative industries export, Russian Export Center JSC

Evgeniya Danilchenko is the Head of creative industries export at the Russian Export Center JSC (REC). REC is a State-owned development institute established by the Government to support the development of the non-commodity exports industry. The Russian Export Center Group incorporates the Russian Agency for Export Credit and Investment Insurance (EXIAR JSC) and Eximbank of Russia JSC aiming to offer comprehensive integrated services to the export-oriented companies.⁠

More than two years ago Evgeniya has set up a new line of business in REC by developing a special program to promote Russian creative industries export.
She also acts as a focal point for interaction with the relevant domestic ministries and agencies and cooperates with key business players in Russia to improve the export conditions in Russia and help bypass trade barriers.
During this time, she initiated at the legislative level a package of state support measures for the creative industry in Russia, which also includes:
• Russian Federal incentives program;
• Special promotion programs of products to present products and content to foreign markets and adapt them to local requirements, patent and IP protection, registration and certification;
• Improvement of regulatory requirements and procedures for export in terms of qualitative improvement of Russia's positions in international rankings and so on.
Previously Evgeniya worked for the main state international projects, including XXII Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games in Sochi 2014, preparation of the 21st FIFA World Cup in Russia and has 3 years’ experience as a project manager for the International Youth Forum in Russia.

Projekt EU53591

Projekt EU53591 „Loomemajanduse arenduskeskus Creative Gate“. Creative Gate on loomemajanduse arenduskeskus, mille eesmärk on arendada Eesti audiovisuaalvaldkonna (fookusega teleproduktsioonil) ettevõtlus- ja ekspordipotentsiaali, pakkudes terviklikku tugiteenuste paketti nii filmivaldkonna ettevõtele kui ka sidusvaldkondade ettevõtetele. Projekti rahastab Euroopa Regionaalarengu Fond summas 200 000 eurot.
Projekti tulemusena on Eesti audiovisuaaltööstus elujõulisem ja rahvusvaheliselt konkunrentsivõimelisem ning Eesti suudab senisest paremini kasutada oma potentsiaali rahvusvahelise võttepaigana.
Loomemajanduse arendamise meedet rahastab Euroopa Regionaalarengu Fond. Rohkem ingo teavitamise kohta leiate siit: https://www.eas.ee/teavitamine/
