European Genre Forum

European Genre Forum is a pan-european project organized by Black Nights Film Festival from Tallinn; Imagine Film Festival from Amsterdam; Fantastic Zagreb Film Festival and film consultancy company Screen Division from Sitges. The main goal of this international project is to connect young and upcoming European film talents.

The European Genre Forum (EGF) is a training programme that sets to boost the careers of future European players in the genre film industry through three talent labs, packed with trainings, panels mentoring and pitching sessions. The Director’s Lab takes place in Amsterdam during the Imagine Film Festival, in April andThe Producer’s Lab takes place in Zagreb during Fantastic Zagreb in July.

The Marketing&Packaging Lab takes place in Tallinn during Industry@Tallinn & Baltic Event, in the final week of November. During the talent labs, the teams of selected film projects will be able to discuss their ideas with script consultants, learn about the different aspects of (co-) production, sales, and packaging from Industry professionals. The programme also includes mentoring sessions with established genre filmmakers, crowd funders, legal experts, and discussions about case studies of recent, successful films to complete the programme.

Projekt EU53591

Projekt EU53591 „Loomemajanduse arenduskeskus Creative Gate“. Creative Gate on loomemajanduse arenduskeskus, mille eesmärk on arendada Eesti audiovisuaalvaldkonna (fookusega teleproduktsioonil) ettevõtlus- ja ekspordipotentsiaali, pakkudes terviklikku tugiteenuste paketti nii filmivaldkonna ettevõtele kui ka sidusvaldkondade ettevõtetele. Projekti rahastab Euroopa Regionaalarengu Fond summas 200 000 eurot.
Projekti tulemusena on Eesti audiovisuaaltööstus elujõulisem ja rahvusvaheliselt konkunrentsivõimelisem ning Eesti suudab senisest paremini kasutada oma potentsiaali rahvusvahelise võttepaigana.
Loomemajanduse arendamise meedet rahastab Euroopa Regionaalarengu Fond. Rohkem ingo teavitamise kohta leiate siit: https://www.eas.ee/teavitamine/
