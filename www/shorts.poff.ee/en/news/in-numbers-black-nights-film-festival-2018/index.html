In Numbers: Black Nights Film Festival 2018
News

The 22nd Black Nights Film Festival was a blast! Here’s an overview how it all went down in numbers:

Number of guests: 1161 (from 69 countries)
Official feature-length submissions: 2400

Number of documentaries screened: 31
Number of screenings: 858
Number of world/international premieres: 23/30
